Responsible Gaming and Compliance

Committed to providing responsible gaming

At Dragonfish, we believe our primary responsibility is to provide the best online gaming entertainment for our partners. However, we also acknowledge the potential danger that gaming may pose for a small minority of people. Therefore, we strive for excellence in our responsible gaming policy and ethical conduct.

Through education, Dragonfish actively raises awareness of gaming issues, and regularly provides staff with the tools and training necessary to ensure a responsible gaming environment. We continuously monitor our policies, generate new innovative ways to foster caring and ethical gaming, and above all, make certain our customers are safe.

There are mandatory entry-level training and weekly refresher courses for all Customer Support staff on Responsible Gambling and techniques to assist in identifying compulsive or underage gambling.

Payment Processing, Risk and Fraud

We provide a full array of global and local online payment solutions in multiple currencies. We also offer leading third party and proprietary systems to perform KYC, AML, risk scoring, age verification and fraud detection protocols.
