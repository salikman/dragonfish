About us

Dragonfish announced it’s new Snow white client program

The Dragonfish Bingo Network is one of the world’s leading bingo networks with over 180 white label brands, all using the state-of-the-art Snow White framework - Angular 9 based technology. This technology provides a fully responsive, single page application using Push technology and delivers an enhanced player experience over web or native apps.

Snow White is a flexible infrastructure, allowing the Dragonfish partners to choose between a Vanilla solution and a fully customized bespoke application.

Companies joining the Dragonfish network also benefit from; UKGC license, instant liquidity, fully customised design, website building and hosting capabilities, 24/7 network management, CRM, ePayments, Fraud & Risk Management, chat hosting and 24/7 customer support.

Dragonfish will also provide a broad selection of Quickplay instant format games including branded slots such as X Factor, table games, virtual sport, Hi Lo and scratch cards, as well as over 70 proprietary games exclusive to 888 and its B2B partners.
